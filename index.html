About Afrocentric Visions

Our story and commitment to authentic representation

Afrocentric Visions was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a platform where authentic Afrocentric art and imagery could flourish while directly supporting the artists who create it. Born from conversations with designers, marketing professionals, and cultural advocates who struggled to find high-quality, culturally respectful imagery, our platform emerged as a solution that bridges the gap between creative professionals seeking meaningful content and talented artists deserving fair compensation and visibility. What started as a passion project has grown into a thriving ecosystem connecting thousands of creators with authentic visual content that celebrates African heritage and contemporary diaspora culture.

At our core, we believe that representation matters. We're committed to democratizing access to high-quality Afrocentric art while ensuring that artists receive fair compensation and recognition for their work. Through transparent licensing practices, artist-first policies, and continuous community engagement, we're not just selling art; we're fostering a movement that recognizes the value and power of African and diaspora creative voices in shaping contemporary visual culture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our collection and services

We offer multiple licensing options tailored to different needs: Personal Use licenses for individual projects, Commercial licenses for business applications, Extended licenses for exclusive rights, and Educational licenses for nonprofits and institutions. Each license clearly outlines permitted uses, attribution requirements, and any restrictions. Our transparent licensing model ensures you know exactly what you're purchasing and how you can use the artwork. All licenses include support from our team to answer specific usage questions.

We continuously add new artwork to our collection with fresh submissions from our artist community. Premium members receive early access to new releases and can set up notifications for specific artists or themes they follow. Our collection grows organically based on artist participation and community feedback. We also feature seasonal collections, artist collaborations, and thematic series throughout the year. You can browse our "New Releases" section to discover the latest additions, and our blog keeps you updated on collection highlights and artist spotlights.

Artist compensation is at the heart of our mission. We operate with a transparent revenue-sharing model where artists receive a significant percentage of every sale. Unlike traditional stock photo platforms, we prioritize artist earnings and provide detailed analytics showing how their work performs. Artists have control over pricing and licensing terms for their work, and we process payments monthly without hidden fees or deductions. We also feature artist spotlights, provide marketing support to help their work gain visibility, and maintain an artist advisory board that shapes our platform policies. Our commitment is ensuring that creators are valued partners, not just content suppliers.
